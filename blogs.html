Blogs

Throughout this year, I've had the opportunity to write several blogs on a variety of topics including developing technologies, how automation will shape the future, and how hacking isn't as mysterious as it might first seem. Below, I'd like to highlight a few of these blogs and posts.

The Future Might Be Scary

Every day there are new cool and amazing advances in technology that could possibly have massive impacts on our future. In this blog, I selected one new technology each week to talk about, explained what it is, what stage of development it's at, and then I gave my predictions about how it could affect our lives in the future. These technologies included new forms of propulsion for space fight, gene sequencing and editing, 3D printed organs, Artificial Intelligence, and much more!
