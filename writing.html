Writing

Over the past few years, there has been a shift in the way that websites are designed, most obviously in the way that mobile websites are designed. The website design industry has moved from the idea of separate websites for computers, phones, and tablets to having one website that molds itself to look good on every screen.

This paper examines the historical context for "pixel-perfect" web design and how the advent of the smartphone along with advances in internet technology led to the shift from separate mobile sites to responsively-designed websites.
